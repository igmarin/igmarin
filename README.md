## 🚀 About me

I am a **Tech Lead and Senior Software Engineer** focused on building AI systems that are reliable, measurable, and deeply integrated into the software development lifecycle. For a full portfolio and detailed case studies, visit [**ismaelmarin.dev**](https://ismaelmarin.dev/).

Formerly at **Dealerware**, I led cross-functional squads, authored RFCs/ADRs, and owned a system processing **10,000+ transactions per hour** with 370+ merged PRs. I’ve transitioned that foundation into **AI Engineering**, where I focus on multi-step agentic workflows, deterministic grounding (MCP), and the "ROI of Context."

---

## 🧠 Philosophy: The "ROI of Context"

My approach to AI Engineering is built on three core pillars:

1.  **Product-Led Engineering:** The engineer who understands the product always wins. Technical skill is a multiplier for product understanding.
2.  **Empirical Rigor (Anti-"Vibe Coding"):** Moving from "it looks right" to objective measurement. AI behavior must be verified through automated evaluation loops, not subjective impressions.
3.  **Deterministic Grounding:** Grounding probabilistic LLMs in deterministic realities via **MCP (Model Context Protocol)** and explicit "receipts" (proof of tests, linters, and architectural compliance).
